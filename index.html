World Street Bicycle Racing Championship

WORLD STREET BICYCLE RACING CHAMPIONSHIP

The rules of the game are outlined below:-

  • Use up-arrow key to jump up.
  • Collect coins to increase wealth.
  • Make sure to collect lives to sustain yourself in the game.
  • Collect energy boosters to boost up your speed.
  • Avoid crashing with the obstacles as it will slow down your speed and use your life.
  • Try to stay up longer in the game to increase your score.
  • You will lose the game if all your lives are utilized.
  • Performance will be remarked on the basis of your score.
  • Click on restart button to play again.
